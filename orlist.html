Open Source

OSERS is hosted entirely on Github, and can be viewed/downloaded with ease. [View on Github]

Accessible to everyone.

Decentralized

OSERS is crowd funded and operated on Open Online Entertainment Forum. Anyone can join and contribute. There is no individual at OOEF that is a part of a solidified central governing body, because it does not exist. Change and action comes in the form of votes, more votes = more change. Change is enacted and accounted by the I.T forum of OOEF.

Operations

OSERS is operated by volunteers who enact and account change. Anyone can request anything, and it is required for it to be voted on. If the vote is successful, the change must be carried out efficiently and effectively by the OSERS Contributors. Become a Contributor.

Compatibility

OSERS is applicable to almost anything entertainment related. Anime, Board Games, Card Games, Cartoons, Comics, Documentaries, Movies, TV Shows, Video Games, and nearly everything else.

M.OSERS will also be available for music rating starting on December 31st, 2021.
