Additional Information

Enforcement: OSERS Ratings are backed by the word of each publisher, respectively. Whom to receive rating, are required to fully disclose all content of their game, software, or any other product, truthfully.

Reporting a Publisher: If a publisher is wrongfully using an OSERS rating emblem on their product, OSERS can pursue legal action. Report a publisher here.
