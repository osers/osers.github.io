- The Five Main OSERS Ratings:

[K] Child

Ages: 0 to 9 years

Any product or software with the [K] rating cannot include Alcohol References, Blood, Cartoon Violence, Comic Mischief, Crude Humor, Fantasy Violence, Tobacco References, Blood and Gore, Drug References, Language, Lyrics, Use of Alcohol, Use of Tobacco, Violence, Violent References, Intense Violence, Mature Humor, Partial Nudity, Simulated Gambling, Strong Language, Strong Lyrics, Suggestive Themes, Use of Drugs, Nudity, Real Gambling, Sexual Content, Sexual Themes, or Sexual Violence


[N] Neutral

Ages: 10 years and older

Any product or software with the [N] rating cannot include Blood and Gore, Drug References, Excessive use of Innapropriate Language, Use of Alcohol, Use of Tobacco, Violence, Violent References, Intense Violence, Mature Humor, Partial Nudity, Simulated Gambling, Strong Language, Strong Lyrics, Suggestive Themes, Use of Drugs, Nudity, Real Gambling, Sexual Content, Sexual Themes, or Sexual Violence

[N] rated products can include Alcohol References, Blood, Cartoon Violence, Comic Mischief, Crude Humor, Fantasy Violence, and Tobacco References


[W] Warmtemp

Ages: 13 years and older

Any product or software with the [W] rating cannot include Intense Violence, Mature Humor, Partial Nudity, Simulated Gambling, Strong Language, Strong Lyrics, Suggestive Themes, Use of Drugs, Nudity, Real Gambling, Sexual Content, Sexual Themes, or Sexual Violence

[W] rated products can include everything [N] rated products can include, plus: Blood and Gore, Drug References, Language, Lyrics, Use of Alcohol, Use of Tobacco, Violence, and Violent References


[M] Mature

Ages: 16 years and older

Any product or software with the [M] rating cannot include Nudity, Real Gambling, Sexual Content, Sexual Themes, or Sexual Violence

[M] rated products include everything [N] and [W] include, along with: Intense Violence, Mature Humor, Partial Nudity, Simulated Gambling, Strong Language, Strong Lyrics, Suggestive Themes, and Use of Drugs


[X] Adult

Ages: 18 years and older

Any product or software with the [X] rating can include everything.

[X] rated products include everything [N], [W], and [M] include, along with: Nudity, Real Gambling, Sexual Content, Sexual Themes, and Sexual Violence


- Combining OSERS Ratings:


[AA] All Ages

Ages: 0 to 199 years

[AA] along with [E] can combine with the five main OSERS ratings to create a more specific rating. For example, a language course may be rated [AAE] due to it being education that all ages can learn. [AAE] is All Ages Education.

[E] Education

Ages: 0 to 199 years

[E] like [AA], can combine with ratings for a more tailored rating. For example, a schoolhouse may assign an online activity for the students to do at home, that online assignment may receive the [KE] rating, for Child [K] Education [E].

Additional Information

(Loose) Definitions: Our Neutral Rating is 'neutral' in terms of being in between Kids [K] and Warmtemp [W]. The Warmtemp rating (formerly Teen [T]) has its name because if 'Hottemp' was Mature - due to the nature of content with [M], [W] is warm when in comparison to [M].
